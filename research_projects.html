Unveiling Microstates: Decoding EEG Patterns in Dementia
Investigating EEG Microstate Analysis for Cognitive State Characterization

Details  

This project proposal outlines a short research endeavor focused on exploring and analyzing EEG signals through the lens of microstate analysis. EEG microstates represent fundamental neural activity patterns that recur over time and are closely tied to cognitive processes. .
